Virtual Mentorships

Our Program:
Confronting difficult situations should never be an individual battle. That's why we created the virtual mentorship program, so individuals fighting challenges can find support from someone else that's been in the same situation. The flexibility of online meetings means that you can determine the time and place that fits your individual schedule. Once you sign up for the program, our team works to find a mentor that will help you face your challenges head on, and become a trusted friend. There are options for larger group interactions as well, depending on your comfort level. Every individual that signs up will have access to countless online resources that can help you fight your battle through discovering new interests and gaining skills that will help you during difficult times. There's no better time to reach out and find a mentor than now!

Our Mentors:
All mentors in the program are individuals that have struggled with similar challenges in the past, and are still fighting today. Mentor applicants are interviewed before becoming a mentor, and our team ensures that each mentor is equipped with mentorship training and resources to assist you in your journey. Each mentor is assigned to an individual based on personality, experience, and availabilty in order to ensure that you are matched with someone that can help you to feel empowered to continue fighting your battles.



Q&A

Anyone struggling with a substance use disorder.
Everyone deserves someone willing to fight in their corner, and one of the best ways to do so is by using our mentorship program. There is no shame in the struggles that you may be facing, and we want to provide the support you deserve, regardless of your race, religious beliefs, or sexual orientation.

Fill out our mentorship request form
Our form is easy to understand and simple to fill out. All you need to do is provide us with basic background information, contact preferences, your schedule availability, and mentor preferences so that our mentorship team can select the best mentor to suit your unique needs.

Nope!
It shouldn't cost money to ask for the help that you deserve. This is why our mentorship program is completely free for program members.
